About Us

Rucker's Steakhouse

It all began when Rucker's was founded in 1975. Arizona has a long and rich history. Come now and experience on our locally owned Rucker's Best Steakhouse in Scottsdale Arizona as we transport you on a wonderful mountainview dining experience.

At Rucker's you can enjoy a qualifty steak, amazing wine, family-friendly and a quiet mountain-themed restaurant. We feature USDA top choice cuts of beef all cooked over our famous open oak-fired frill. Our menu offers a wide variety of wine, appetizers and entrees along with nightly specials including our perfectly seasoned, T-bone, Porterhouse, Sirlion and fork tender prime rib of beef served every Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday evenings. See the sunset on our outdoor wraparound porch. Rucker's Steakhouse is open 7 days a week. See you soon!
